About Q-Tech Industriess

Q-Tech Industriess is a TUV ISO 9001:2015 certified machine successfully operating since 1999. Our 10,000 sq.ft facility in Puducherry houses state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities across 4,500 sq.ft of plant area.

We specialize in precision manufacturing of ferrous & non-ferrous critical components for both automotive and non-automotive sectors, including hot forged, cold forged, cast iron, and pressure die casting parts.

Our dedicated team of qualified operators and quality inspectors, supported by modern measuring equipment, ensures efficient output in both quantity and quality.

Quality Assurance

At Q-Tech Industries, quality is at the core of everything we do. Our motto is to work with perfection to meet and satisfy customer requirements by providing "On Time Delivery in Full (OTIF) with Excellent quality products against customer requirements".

We maintain a comprehensive quality management system certified to TUV ISO 9001:2015 standards. Our measuring instruments are properly maintained and calibrated in third-party NABL accreditation laboratories to ensure measurement accuracy and traceability.

Our quality policy ensures that every component we manufacture meets the highest standards of precision and reliability, backed by our team of experienced quality inspectors and modern measuring equipment.
